ABOUT DORAEMON(ANIME-2005)



Doraemon is a Japanese Manga series written and illustrated by Fujiko Fujio. The series had also been adapted into a succesful anime series. The story revolves into a earless robotic cat named Doraemon, who mainly travels back in time from the 22nd century to support a boy named Nobita Nobi. This series is published by Shogakukan.



About the animated series:


In the episodes there are 5 main characters. They are - Nobita Nobi, Doraemon, Shizuka Nobi, Gian Goda and Suneo Honekawa. The outer characters are - Nobita's mother, Nobita's father, Gian's mother, Suneo's mother, Shizuka's mother, Nobita's teacher.

In the normal episodes Gian and Suneo teases Nobita; so Nobita comes to Doraemon for help, as a result Doraemon gives him a gadget. Then Nobita takes that gadget hapilly. In other Episodes Nobita goes to the school and gives test and comes back woth"0" on his marksheet at home. As a result his mother (Tamako Nobi) scolds Nobita.
